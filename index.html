How It Works

Facts

* Dental care fees in Latin American are only a tenth to a quarter of what the same services would cost in North America and Europe.

* Ecuador is becoming a hub for dental tourism, given that most of the common procedures can be taken care of at an equivalent level of quality as North American and European counterparts, but at a fraction of the cost.

* Many dentists in Ecuador’s most important cities have been educated in the U.S., are board certified, are bilingual, and are continuously undergoing continuing education. State-of-the-art equipment and modern offices can provide additional comfort for those who view the trip to the dentist as a kind of medieval torture session.

Services

* We make high quality, affordable health care from top local providers available to travelers.

* We help in making your trip to Ecuador the perfect opportunity to get that nuance with your teeth out of the way.

* We let you book in advance for any simple dental treatment you may be needing. You just need a couple hours from your time in any major Ecuadorian city to come out with a fabulous new smile.

* We provide information on the cost, location, credentials, and reviews of the service by different dental care providers, letting you choose the alternative that makes you feel comfortable.

* We provide transportation to and from the dental facility where you have booked your service.

* We ensure that you are fully satisfied with the dental treatment you have selected, and that you the whole experience matches or exceeds your expectations.

* We let you make the most of your trip by saving big money on your dental treatment, meeting a local friendly face, and getting to experience Ecuadorian hospitality first hand.

* Stroll'nSmile is an online platform that makes available to those visiting Ecuador, the excellent and affordable dental care services available to locals.

Market Place

We let customers easily find out about the location, facilities, credentials, and cost of the services by different dentists and clinics, and make a booking at their convenience.

Ratings

Travelers can leave their comments and rate the service received, and also learn about the experiences of other travelers.

Just Book and Relax

Once you book, we will pick you up from your hotel, hostal, or wherever you are accommodated, and will drive you back there once your dental appointment is successfully completed.

24/7 Support

Our team members are always there for you in rain, snow and sunshine.

Location
